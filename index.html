Welcome to CAPTCHR


A speculative Net.Art experience that explores how CAPTCHAs define the boundary between human and computer.

CAPTCHAs require humans to prove that they're not robots by passing tests designed to be impossible for computers.

The answers to these tests are used to build databases that allow computers to gain more human-like abilities.

As computers become more capable at completing human tasks, what will future CAPTCHAs test and what kind of human data will they capture?
